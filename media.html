Ceramic and Copper

But why are ceramic and copper less popular kitchen choices?

There are many advantages to using ceramic and copper frying pans.

In the case of Ceramic:

  • Ceramic is similar to nonstick
  • It's lightweight and easy to use
  • Easily cleans with soap and water

On the other hand, Copper:

  • Excellent heat conduction
  • Copper pans are very durable
  • They look beautiful!
