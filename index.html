Welcome to Srebro.ca.

Srebro is a new and small Canadian company that offers a wide selection of beautiful frying pans and skillets. Each is made with attention to detail and care in Ontario, Canada.

These are premium items available in various materials like:

  • Nonstick

  • Stainless Steel

  • Cast Iron

  • Ceramic

  • Copper

Srebro has many brands like Srebro Millenia, Essentials and Mini. Ordering online is easy, and you can find us in stores!
