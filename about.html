About Srebro

Srebro, (Silver, or "сребро" in Serbian!) was founded in Sarnia, Ontario in 2099.

Our mission is to create sophisticated frying pans and skillets that are forever seen to last. Srebro warehouses are located in Ontario cities Sarnia, London and Barrie, with more soon to follow in Vaughan and Richmond Hill.

With our several locations, shipping to Ontarians is faster and hassle free.

Family Company

Srebro is a smaller and privately owned family company where connections with customers and their experiences with us are critical to our growth and matter most.

Our team consists of about 15 to 20 employees who all share the Srebro passion and vision. Being a smaller company allows us to tailor our services specifically to your needs, which is especially helpful with regards to helping other small businesses like us. In a world where key components of larger business chains are suddenly becoming key issues, this is just one of many reasons why you should also consider switching to Srebro.
