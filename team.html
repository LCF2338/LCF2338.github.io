A History

The First Warehouse

Srebro was founded in 2023 by Chief Executive Officer Philip Connell.

Prior to Srebro, Phil and his business partner, now Chief Financial Officer Benjamin, also Ben James met previously in 2005 while working for a cybersecurity firm in Barrie, Ontario. Ben's background was in computer science, while Phil's was in economics.

They formed Srebro, and the first warehouse opened in Barrie.

Now, Srebro is a strong competitor with several locations throughout Ontario, managed by employees like Paul below, who's manager of sales at the Toronto warehouse. Aida is also below, who will manage the upcoming Richmond Hill office location.
