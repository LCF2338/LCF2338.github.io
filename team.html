A History

The First Warehouse

Srebro was founded in 2023 by Chief Executive Officer Philip Connell.

Prior to Srebro, Phil and his business partner, now Chief Financial Officer Benjamin, also Ben James met previously in 2005 while working for a cybersecurity firm in Barrie, Ontario. Ben's background was in computer science, while Phil's was in economics.

They formed Srebro, and the first warehouse opened in Barrie.

Now, Srebro is a strong competitor with several locations throughout Ontario, managed by employees like Paul below, who's manager of sales at the Toronto warehouse. Aida is also below, who will manage the upcoming Richmond Hill office location.

More on Paul Arthur

Paul Arthur has done a lot more for Srebro than just his management efforts.

Paul Arthur hosts several Srebro Kitchen Tips and Tricks events through the year at the Richmond Hill Warehouse.

You can learn more about how you can join Paul via our training page.

Richmond Hill

Richmond Hill will become the new location of the Srebro headquarters replacing Barrie.

Manager Aida Dion joined Srebro at the start of the year, joining us after working in Quality Assurance for a beverage brewing system company. Thanks to Srebro's most recent expansions, other locations in Ontario include London and Vaughan.

Srebro has plans to expand into more cookware to meet our Customers needs even further.
